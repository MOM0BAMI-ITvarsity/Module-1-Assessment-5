Black Widow

Bio

Full Name: Natasha Romanoff

Place Of Birth: Stalingrad, Soviet Union (now Volgograd, Russia)

Alignment: Good

Gender: Female

Race: Human


Power Stats

Intelligence: (8/10) Natasha Romanoff is a highly intelligent and cunning spy with a knack for espionage.

Strength: (6/10) While not superhuman, Black Widow's training gives her above-average strength.

Speed: (9/10) Black Widow is agile and quick, making her a formidable adversary in combat.

Durability: (7/10) Her agility and reflexes contribute to her ability to avoid harm.

Power: (7/10) Black Widow is a master of various martial arts and specializes in covert operations.

Combat: (10/10) Highly skilled in hand-to-hand combat, Black Widow is an expert in both armed and unarmed fighting.


Additional Info

Alias: Black Widow is also known as "The Silent Comedian" among her teammates for her unexpected humor.

Fun Fact: Black Widow has a collection of vintage spy gadgets that she uses for practical jokes.

Guilty Pleasure: Black Widow's guilty pleasure is binge-watching romantic comedies, especially those set in the '80s.

Comic Book Fan: She collects vintage comic books and can recite the origin stories of obscure superheroes.
