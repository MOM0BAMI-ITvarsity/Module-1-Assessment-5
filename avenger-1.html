Thor

Bio

Full Name: Thor Odinson

Place Of Birth: Asgard

Alignment: Good

Gender: Male

Race: Asgardian


Power Stats

Intelligence: (8/10) Thor possesses a divine intellect, although he sometimes relies on his brawn over brains.

Strength: (10/10) As the God of Thunder, Thor's strength is off the charts, allowing him to wield his enchanted hammer, Mjolnir.

Speed: (9/10) Thor can travel at incredible speeds, especially when riding his trusty winged horse, Sleipnir.

Durability: (9/10) His Asgardian physiology grants him high resistance to physical harm.

Power: (10/10) Thor commands the power of lightning and storms, using Mjolnir to unleash devastating attacks.

Combat: (10/10) Trained in various Asgardian combat styles, Thor is a formidable warrior.


Additional Info

Hobbies: Besides saving the universe, Thor enjoys playing Asgardian rock music on his enchanted guitar, "Mjolnirstrings."

Fun Fact: Thor once tried to challenge Hulk to a dance-off but quickly realized it was a "smashing" failure.

Favorite Food: Thor has a soft spot for Midgardian ice cream, particularly the flavor "Thunder Crunch Delight."

Pet Peeve: He's annoyed when people mispronounce Mjolnir; it's "Mee-ol-neer," not "Muh-jol-nar."
