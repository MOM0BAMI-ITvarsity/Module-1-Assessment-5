Captain America

Bio

Full Name: Steve Rogers

Place Of Birth: Brooklyn, New York

Alignment: Good

Gender: Male

Race: Human


Power Stats

Intelligence: (8/10) Captain America possesses tactical brilliance and strategic thinking.

Strength: (8/10) Super Soldier Serum enhances Cap's physical abilities, making him peak human in strength.

Speed: (9/10) Captain America is an agile and fast combatant, utilizing his enhanced reflexes.

Durability: (8/10) The Super Soldier Serum enhances Cap's endurance and resilience.

Power: (8/10) Cap's iconic shield is not just a defensive tool; he wields it with precision as an offensive weapon.

Combat: (9/10) Trained in various martial arts and armed combat, Captain America is a master tactician.


Additional Info

Superpower: Besides super strength, Captain America has an uncanny ability to always find the last slice of pizza.

Fun Fact: Captain America secretly loves karaoke and often belts out tunes from the '40s.

Dance Style: Captain America is surprisingly skilled at ballroom dancing and once won a dance competition with his partner, Black Widow.

Gadget Collection: Cap secretly collects vintage gadgets, and his favorite is an antique typewriter.
