Hulk

Bio

Full Name: Bruce Banner

Place Of Birth: Ohio

Alignment: Good

Gender: Male

Race: Human (Radiation)


Power Stats

Intelligence: (7/10) Bruce Banner possesses a brilliant scientific mind, but his intelligence is often overshadowed by the Hulk's brute force.

Strength: (11/10) Hulk's strength is virtually limitless, allowing him to overpower almost any opponent.

Speed: (7/10) Despite his massive size, Hulk can move surprisingly fast, especially when angered.

Durability: (10/10) The Hulk's skin and muscles provide unparalleled resistance to harm.

Power: (10/10) Hulk's power lies in his ability to generate extreme levels of gamma radiation, fueling his incredible transformations.

Combat: (6/10) While Hulk relies on raw power, he has developed some combat techniques for efficiency.


Additional Info

Hobbies: In his free time, Hulk enjoys poetry writing and has a soft spot for haikus.

Fun Fact: Hulk once participated in a hot dog eating contest and won without turning green.

Favorite Book: Hulk's favorite bedtime story is "Green Eggs and Ham" by Dr. Seuss.

Soothing Ritual: When Hulk gets stressed, he practices yoga to maintain his zen state.
